# YouTube Channel Performance Analysis 📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the performance of a gaming YouTube channel (Khalofa) over 4 years (2023-2026). The goal was to uncover patterns in viewer retention, revenue growth, and content effectiveness, transitioning from basic reporting to predictive analytics.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
* **Power BI:** Data visualization, interactive dashboards, and AI-driven forecasting.
* **DAX:** Created complex measures for Month-over-Month (MoM) growth, Year-to-Date (YTD) calculations, and dynamic categorization.
* **Data Source:** YouTube Analytics raw data (Excel/CSV).

## 💡 Key Insights Extracted
1. **The Shorts vs. Long-form Gap:** Analyzed 11M impressions vs. 9M views to identify content gaps and viewer behavior (Shorts dominance vs. long-form profitability).
2. **Revenue Optimization:** Discovered that despite millions of views in 2025, revenue was disproportionately low. Strategic adjustments in 2026 improved the RPM significantly.
3. **Seasonality Impact:** Proved that Q1 and Q4 are the peak seasons for this gaming channel, requiring aggressive content scheduling, while Q2 and Q3 need alternative content strategies.
4. **Predictive Forecasting:** Utilized Power BI's built-in AI to project subscriber and revenue growth through 2026, defining best-case and worst-case scenarios.
